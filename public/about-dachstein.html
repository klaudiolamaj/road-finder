About Dachstein
natural paradise of Austria

Your're here : Home / About Dachstein

Welcome to Dachstein, Austria – a haven for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike! Situated in the heart of the Austrian Alps, Dachstein is a captivating destination boasting a diverse array of activities and landscapes that cater to every taste. Winter Wonderland In the winter, Dachstein transforms into a skier's paradise with its pristine slopes and world-class skiing facilities. The Dachstein Glacier, one of the most iconic features, ensures a long skiing season, offering enthusiasts the chance to carve through the snow against the backdrop of breathtaking alpine scenery. Year-Round Adventure Beyond skiing, Dachstein offers a plethora of year-round activities. During the warmer months, the region's hiking trails open up to reveal stunning alpine meadows, tranquil lakes, and jaw-dropping vistas. The network of trails caters to all levels, from leisurely strolls to challenging hikes, providing a unique perspective on the region's natural beauty. Cultural Charm Immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage of Dachstein. The charming villages dotted around the region exude a traditional Austrian charm. Explore local markets, sample authentic cuisine in rustic chalets, and experience the warm hospitality of the locals. Dachstein Ice Cave One of the crown jewels of Dachstein is the Dachstein Ice Cave, a captivating underground world of ice formations. Guided tours take visitors through a maze of stunning stalactites and stalagmites, offering a surreal experience beneath the surface of the glacier. Thrilling Skywalk For the daring adventurer, the Dachstein Skywalk awaits. This suspension bridge extends over the edge of the mountain, providing unparalleled views of the Dachstein Glacier and the surrounding peaks. It's an adrenaline-pumping experience that adds an extra layer of excitement to your visit. Accommodations and Culinary Delights Dachstein ensures a comfortable stay with a range of accommodations, from cozy alpine chalets to luxurious hotels. Indulge in traditional Austrian cuisine, savoring local specialties that reflect the region's culinary excellence. Discover the allure of Dachstein, Austria – a destination where natural beauty, adventure, and cultural richness converge to create an unforgettable experience for every visitor.
